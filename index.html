We're glad you're here

We offer the best in school transport services to school going children within the Nairobi area. Our transport vehicles are compliant with the National Transport Safety Authority regulations of Kenya, ensuring that the safety of your child at all times.

Services

Below are some of the services we offer.

School Transport

We offer transport services to day schooling students and staff in Nairobi. Individuals are transported to school and back home under the care of our qualified staff.

Car Hire

Planning for a trip somwhere? Contact us for the fairest rates for our vehicles. we not only provide you with a vehicle, but a driver and conductor as well.

Custom Packages

We provide customized transport servicess for organizations and corporate institutios that wish to offer transport for their staff on daily basis.
