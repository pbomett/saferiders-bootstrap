A one-stop-shop service

Whether you are looking to get into the school transport business but not sure where to start or you are looking to increase capacity for your school transport operations, we have the right tools to help you scale your business. We provide a unique set of solutions that target school transporters of all classes.

What we offer

Student Subscriptions

Got empty seats in your transport vehicle? We assist operating transporters fill up vaccant seats by linking them up with new student subscriptions.

Fleet Management

We offer management and logistical support for fleets to move students seamlessly from pick up to drop off points.

Statutory Documents

Paperwork can be daunting, We help you get done with the right paperwork the right way so you can get down to business as faster and fully compliant..

Staffing and Staff Management

Finding the right talent for the job can be difficult. We don't just find the right people, but we train them on the job.

Routes Planning

With our expertise, we will help you map out the best route for your vehicle to maximize efficiency, saving your resources.

Pricing and Payments Processing

We negotiate for best prices with the students who subscribe through our platform. We manage students accounts and process all payments directly to your account.
